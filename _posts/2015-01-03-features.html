For the physician with a fast paced lifestyle

Helps you collect billing info on the fly, on any mobile device, or on your computer.

"Which code am I supposed to use..."

You have 2 weeks of daily visit codes to enter? You don't have to enter it one by one. Smart logic imbedded in billOHIP.ca can quickly enter all the billing codes you need saving you time and ensure you don't forget any day/codes.

Argh... I don't know where I left that pile of billing papers!

The information you enter will be securely saved on a cloud server and will be available anywhere. When you lose your device, there is no worry about exposing sensitive personal health information.

Bank Grade Security

To maintain the highest standards of security and safety of your data we use SSL bank grade encryption. You do not have to worry about exposure of confidential patient information as there are no papers that can be left behind. If you lose your mobile device, your information will be protected as the data is saved on the server rather than on your mobile device.
