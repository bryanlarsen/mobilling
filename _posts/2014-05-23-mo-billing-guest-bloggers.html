Date written: 07/05/2014

The best type of person to provide us with insight and knowledge on the most common medical billing mistakes is the person that processes medical billing for a living, also known as a billing agent. Mo-Billing will be starting a series where guests with useful medical billing insights will be invited to blog and share their information.

 

Our guest bloggers will be introduced and listed on our blog, along with the blogs they have contributed to Mo-Billing.
