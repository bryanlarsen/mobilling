As we continue with our series on Mo-Billing 2.0 features, next we discuss the Mo-Billing messaging system. The messaging system is intended to allow the doctor and agent pair to simply communicate by sending messages attached to a claim through the application. With bank grade security all messages are secure to highest degree. </span></p>

Often times doctors use email to ask questions or give directions to their billing agent, however with email's limited security, doctors are taking a risk in sharing patient information. With Mo-Billing, all information is encrypted and protected in a high security data center. </span>Furthermore, there may be confusion in deciphering to which claim the doctor is referring to through email messaging so avoid emails that look like this: </span></p>

Doctor: "Can you add A007 to J. A.'s claim?" </span></p>

Billing Agent: "Which J. A.? On which admission? What date?"</span></p>

Doctor: "Well I don't remember because I handed over all my billing record to you!"</span></p>


</span>Use Mo-Billing so your messages are attached directly to the claim you are referring to avoiding any confusion and delays, subsequently saving time. </span></p>
