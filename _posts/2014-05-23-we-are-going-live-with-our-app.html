We are waiting for Apple to review our app and will soon be available on iTunes for download for iPhones and IPads.

 

The app is very easy to use. You can provide us with the patient information either by taking a quick photo of a patient card or by typing in the name, DOB and OHIP number. Added features are searcheable hospital numbers and diagnosis/billing codes. The app is currently optimized for hospitalists who will find auto-generation of billing codes time saving. You enter the first date and the last date on which the patient was seen and the app will automatically generate all the billing codes in between.

 

The app is secured with 258 bit SSL. The data is kept on a secure server so there is no worry even if you lose your smartphone - confidential data will not be divulged!

 

We are looking forward to feedback!
