How to use

billOHIP is a free billing software that can be used to record and create submission files to submit your billing to the Ministry of Health. The software can be used for two cases where:

You do your own billing:

Use billOHIP to electronically record and create submission files compatible with MC-EDT. The submission files created must be uploaded to MC-EDT for submission to the Ministry of Health.

You have a billing agent:

Use billOHIP together with your billing agent, both of which will have an account. Use billOHIP to electronically record and submit your billing information to your billing agent. Your billing agent can review your billing data and create submission files compatible with MC-EDT. The submission files created must be uploaded to MC-EDT for submission to the Ministry of Health.
