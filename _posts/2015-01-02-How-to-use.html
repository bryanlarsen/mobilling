billOHIP.ca is a free billing software that can be used to record and submit your billing to the Ministry of Health.

The software can be used in two ways:

You do your own billing:

1. Use billOHIP.ca to electronically record your billing data.

2. Then create submission files compatible with MC-EDT with billOHIP.ca.

3. Upload the submission files via MC-EDT.

4. Remittance advice or other error messages from the ministry can be reconciled via billOHIP.ca as well!

You have a billing agent:

1. Use billOHIP.ca together with your billing agent. First gather your billing data with billOHIP.ca.

2. Your agent will have an instant access to all your data.

3. Your agent will review your claims.

4. Your agent will submit to the ministry of health and handle reconcilliation as necessary.
