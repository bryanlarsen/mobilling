Are you a billing agent interested in using billOHIP.ca?

billOHIP.ca is a full fledged billing software:

Your client also uses billOHIP.ca:

1. Your client will Use billOHIP.ca to electronically record billing data. You will have instant access to these data.

2. Review the claims and make necessary changes. Once satisfied, let billOHIP.ca create a submission file.

3. Upload the submission files via MC-EDT.

4. Reconcile with billOHIP.ca. Download remittance advice or other error messages from MC-EDT and plug them to billOHIP.ca.

Your client uses paper/fax/email:

First let your client know that using paper/fax/email is inefficienct and insecure!

You will have to manually enter billing information into billOHIP.ca. After that you use billOHIP.ca to create submission file and reconcile!
